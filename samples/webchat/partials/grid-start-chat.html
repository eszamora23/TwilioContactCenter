How the demo works (simple)

  1. Start the chat here as the customer. It routes to the next available agent.
  2. Open the Agent Portal (below) in another window and log in with the demo values.
  3. Reply to yourself from the portal to see the real-time conversation.
  4. If enabled, click Video in the chat toolbar to switch to a video call.
  5. Use Phone Lab to place a test phone call that follows your IVR and routes to the agent.
