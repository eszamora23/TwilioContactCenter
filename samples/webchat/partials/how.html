How it works — plain English

  • Start: You type here as a customer. The next available agent sees it instantly.
  • Routing: The system automatically sends each chat or call to the right agent.
  • Calls: Phone Lab places a test call through your IVR and into the agent’s softphone.
  • Video (optional): You can promote a chat to a video call when enabled.
  • Agent Portal: The agent replies, views customer context, and handles the conversation end-to-end.
